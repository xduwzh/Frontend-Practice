Yuxi You

Yuxi You, Author of fronted framework Vue.js . Builder of HTML5 Clear, independent open source developer. Worked at Google Creative Labs and Meteor Development Group. He started his open-source career because of massive contact with open source project JavaScript. Now delves into development and maintainance of Vue.js.

Study Experience

Yuxi You graduated from Shanghai Fudan Middle School, and completed his bachelor's degree from Colgate University, the US. Later he obtained Master degree of Art of Design & Technology from Parsons College, and work at Google Creative Lab, New York.

Main Achievements

Yuxi You majored in Interior Art and Art History instead of Computer Science during his bachelor. And during his master's study, he met JavaScript and got attracted, which started his Frontend Developement career.

In February 2014, he developed frontend development framework Vue.js. Vue.js is a JavaScript framework for buiding Web UI, which provide clear API and effecient data binding and flexible components.

Membership

In September 3rd 2016, on the JSConf of Nanjing, Yuxi You announced joining Weex team of Alibaba, and he claimed that he would work as a consultant for integrating Vue and Weex's JavaScript runtime, which aims at cross-platform function of Vue.
